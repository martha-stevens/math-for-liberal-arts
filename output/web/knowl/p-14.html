Percent literally means “per 100,” or “parts per hundred.” When we write 40%, this is equivalent to the fraction \(\frac{40}{100}\) or the decimal 0.40. Notice that 80 out of 200 and 10 out of 25 are also 40%, since
\begin{equation*} \frac{80}{200} = \frac{10}{25} = \frac{40}{100} \end{equation*}
