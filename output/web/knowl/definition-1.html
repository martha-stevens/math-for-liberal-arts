Definition 1.1.3. Percents.

If we have a part that is some percent of a whole, then
\begin{equation*} \text{percent}=\frac{\text{part}}{\text{whole}} \end{equation*}
or equivalently,
\begin{equation*} \text{part} = \text{percent} \cdot \text{whole} \end{equation*}
To do the calculations, we write the percent as a decimal.
