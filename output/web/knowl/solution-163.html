Solution 9.4.9.1.

Continuing the pattern of the previous example, the answer should be
P(shared birthday)=\(1-\frac{365}{365} \cdot \frac{364}{365} \cdot \frac{363}{365} \cdot \frac{362}{365}\cdot \frac{361}{365} \approx 0.0271\)
Note that we could rewrite this more compactly as P(shared birthday)= \(1-\frac{_{365}P_{5}}{365^5}\approx 0.0271\) which makes it a bit easier to type into a calculator or computer, and which suggests a nice formula as we continue to expand the population of our group.
