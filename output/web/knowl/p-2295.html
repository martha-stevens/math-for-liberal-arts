Let's calculate the probability of Case A. The probability that the first card is the Ace of Diamonds is \(|frac{1}{52}\text{.}\) The probability that the second card is black given that the first card is the Ace of Diamonds is \(\frac{26}{51}\) because 26 of the remaining 51 cards are black. The probability is therefore \(\frac{1}{52}\cdot \frac{26}{51}=\frac{1}{102}\text{.}\)
