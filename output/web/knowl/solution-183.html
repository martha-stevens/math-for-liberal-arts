Solution 10.3.1.1.

To find the z-score we need to divide the difference between the value, 27, and the mean, 24, by the standard deviation of the set, 2.
\begin{equation*} z = \frac{27 - 24}{2} = \frac{3}{2} \end{equation*}
The z-score of 27 is \(z = 1.5\text{.}\) This indicates that 27 is 1.5 standard deviations above the mean.
