Item 9.7.6.b.

There are \(1326\) ways we can draw two cards from a deck (see previous part of the problem). There are \(_{13}C_2\) ways we can draw two hearts.
\begin{equation*} _{13}C_2 = \frac{13!}{2!11!} = \frac{13\cdot 12}{2\cdot 1} = 78 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} P\left(E\right) = \frac{78}{1326} = 0.0588 \end{equation*}
