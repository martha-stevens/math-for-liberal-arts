Definition 1.3.3. Volumes of Basic Shapes.

The formulas for volumes of rectangular boxes and cylinders are below.
The volume of a rectangular box with height, \(H\text{,}\) width, \(W\text{,}\) and length, \(L\) is:
\begin{equation*} V = LWH \end{equation*}
The volume of a cylinder with radius, \(r\) and height, \(h\) is:
\begin{equation*} V = \pi r^2 h \end{equation*}
